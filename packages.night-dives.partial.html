Night Dives

Night diving is something you should try. The underwater world is spectacular at night! Diving under water at night differs from day programs not only by the illumination, but also by various marine life. All daytime fish freeze in a dream near the reefs or at the bottom, and predators go on a night hunt.

The package includes: transfer from the hotel and back, snacks, drinks and fruits on the ship, 12 liter air tanks and cargo belts, all necessary equipment.
