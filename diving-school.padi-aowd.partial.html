PADI Advanced Open Water Diver (AOWD)

The PADI Advanced Open Water Diver course consists of 5 dives, two of which are mandatory - a deep sea dive (no deeper than 30m) and underwater navigation. The remaining 3 dives are selected at the discretion of the student. It can be: AWARE fish identification, ship diving, towing dives, current diving, mastery of buoyancy, night dive, multilevel dives and computer dives, search and recovery of sunken objects, underwater naturalist, underwater photography, wreck diving.

  • Quantity of dives: 5

  • Approximate Duration: 2 days

  • Prices starting from:
    300.00 USD (Adult)
    200.00 USD (Child)

  • Minimum Age: 10 years old

Experienced instructors: Learn to be a confident scuba diver from a friendly and highly qualified team of trainers.

Practice: The course includes 5 dives on different depth with implementation of exercises and exam passing.

Diving from childhood: the course is available for kids from 10 y.o. For students aged 10 to 12 years, the maximum diving depth is 12m.
