3 Caves - 3 Days

The islands of Pi-Phi are like a small archipelago. The entire coastal strip of this province, along with countless islands along it, is limestone, which means karst. Nobody knows how many caves are located there, the rocks adjoin the sea and go into it, hundreds of islands along the coast are the tops of these rocks, and this whole mountainous country is penetrated by caves, both in the sea and on the coast.

The package includes: transfer from the hotel and back, snacks, drinks and fruits on the ship, 12 liter air tanks and cargo belts, all necessary equipment.
