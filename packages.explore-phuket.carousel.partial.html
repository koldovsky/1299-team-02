DISCOVER SCUBA DIVING

This introductory course is the most comfortable and convenient way to experience diving and scuba diving. Under the guidance of an experienced instructor, master a number of skills that will be credited to you during your training.

OPEN WATER DIVER

The PADI OPEN WATER DIVER course is the basis of all further amateur diving training. The course will teach you the correct use of equipment, help you master diving techniques, and introduce you to the partnership system.

WRECK DIVER

Diving on sunken objects undoubtedly arouses the greatest interest, but also requires special knowledge and training. Labyrinths made of metal structures can become very dangerous for an unqualified diver.
